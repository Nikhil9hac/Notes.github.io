  <p class="question">What is Technolthon</p>
                <p class="answer">Technothlon is an international school championship organized by the student fraternity of IIT Guwahati. Technothlon was started with a group of students in a small room in 2004 to inspire young minds. The competition started with students in the city of Guwahati.
                Later, it expanded to more than 400 cities in the past 14 years with various centres abroad.Now Technothlon spans its wings in 30+ countries internationally. Technothlon is not just an exam but it is an enthralling journey on the path of logic which will test your
                logical and creative thinking to its limit. The championship is organized over 2 rounds</p>
